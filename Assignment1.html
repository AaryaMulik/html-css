Types of Lists

1.Ordered Lists

Ordered lists are the lists which need a specific order. For Example :

  1. cookies 'n' cream
  2. choco chips
  3. Choco brownie

2.Unordered lists

Unordered lists are the lists which do not need any specific order or can be replaceable. For example :

  • Dutch Truffle
  • Red velvet
  • Swiss Chocolate

3.Nested Lists

Nested lists are the lists combined within another lists. For example:

  1. Icecreams
    • cookies 'n' cream
    • choco chips
    • Choco brownie
  2. Cakes
    • Dutch Truffle
    • Red velvet
    • Swiss Chocolate

4.Use of emojis (smiley face and Thumbs up) as bullets

We can use emojis such as smiley face and thumbs up instead of bullets and numbering.For example:

  • 😀 Tea
  • 😀 Coffee
  • 😀 Juice
  • 😀 Milk
